Projects

  • AI Powered Medical Diagnosis: Revolutionizing health care through Automated Image Analysis

    The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) in medical diagnosis is transforming the healthcare landscape, particularly through automated image analysis. This paper presents a web-based application that allows users to submit medical images, which are then pre-processed to be compatible with machine learning models. The application utilizes advanced algorithms to analyze the images and provide predictions regarding potential illnesses. By streamlining the diagnostic process, the app not only enhances the accuracy of medical assessments but also expedites the delivery of care to patients. The application is designed to be user-friendly, catering to both healthcare professionals and individuals seeking to evaluate their symptoms. Upon submission of an image, the app processes the data and communicates with a machine learning model to generate a forecast of the most likely illness. This forecast is then displayed to the user, along with actionable guidance on preventive measures or treatment options. The ability to classify medical images efficiently is crucial, as it can significantly impact patient outcomes and healthcare efficiency. The paper emphasizes the potential of machine learning models to assist healthcare practitioners in making quicker and more accurate diagnoses, ultimately revolutionizing the way healthcare is delivered. In summary, this research highlights the importance of automated image analysis in medical diagnostics and its potential to improve healthcare services through enhanced accuracy and efficiency.


  • Cybersecurity Web App: Implementing AES, DES, Hill Cipher and Caesar Cipher Techniques

    Data protection has never been as important in the modern digital world. The Flask framework and various cryptographic algorithms like the Caesar, Hill, DES, and AES (Advanced Encryption Standard) ciphers are implemented in this web-based encryption and decryption application in this project. Through the friendly UI of the application, plaintext can be transformed into secure ciphertext and decoded again into readable text by users. Each encryption method provides differing levels of security and complexity, enabling individuals to understand and utilize simple and complex cryptographic algorithms. This project demonstrates the practical application of secure data transfer and encryption by bringing together mathematical operations and Python-based cryptography modules. Its effective use is an important milestone towards understanding cybersecurity fundamentals and designing secure online applications. Future releases may involve incorporating additional encryption techniques and enhancing the user experience and security of the app.
